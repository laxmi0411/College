Eligibility (summary)

  • Resident of Bihar with valid address proof.
  • Passed 10+2 (PCM preferred); admitted to recognised college.
  • Age and attempt limits as per scheme rules.

Documents

  • Aadhaar, PAN (student/guardian), photo, signature
  • 10th/12th marksheets, admission letter/fee structure
  • Residence/caste/income certificate (if applicable)
  • Bank details, mobile number, email ID

Process (how to apply)

  1. Register online on the BSCC portal.
  2. Fill application, upload documents, select course/college.
  3. Verification & counselling at District Registration & Counselling Centre (DRCC).
  4. Loan sanction & disbursal to college as per fee demands.
