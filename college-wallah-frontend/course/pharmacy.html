Pharmacy Admission FAQ

What is the eligibility for B.Pharm in India?

10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics/Biology, minimum 50% marks (45% for SC/ST/OBC), and age between 17-23 years.

Which entrance exam is required for pharmacy courses?

GPAT for M.Pharm, state CETs for B.Pharm/D.Pharm, NEET for Pharm.D in some states. Some private colleges have their own entrance tests.
