Law Admission Process in India

Step-by-step guide to secure admission in top law colleges and universities across India.

1

Choose Law Course

Select your preferred law course such as BA LLB, BBA LLB, LLB, or LLM based on eligibility and career goals.

2

Check Eligibility

Ensure you meet eligibility criteria like 10+2 for 5-year courses or graduation for 3-year LLB programs.

3

Appear for Entrance Exams

Appear for exams like CLAT, AILET, LSAT India, or university-level law entrance tests.

4

Application & Counseling

Apply to law colleges and participate in centralized or university-level counseling processes.

5

Seat Allotment

Seats are allotted based on entrance exam rank, merit list, category, and availability.

6

Document Verification

Submit academic certificates, entrance scorecard, ID proof, and category documents if applicable.

7

Fee Payment

Pay admission fees to confirm your seat and complete enrollment formalities.

8

Begin Law Education

Start your law journey with classroom learning, moot courts, internships, and legal training.
