Fashion Design Admission Process in India

Step-by-step guide to secure admission in top fashion design colleges across India.

1

Explore Fashion Programs

Choose your preferred fashion design course such as B.Sc Fashion Design, B.Des, M.Sc, Diploma, or B.F.Tech based on creativity and career goals.

2

Check Eligibility

Ensure eligibility criteria such as 10+2 qualification, minimum marks, age limits, and portfolio (if required).

3

Prepare Portfolio & Exams

Prepare a creative portfolio and get ready for entrance exams like NIFT, NID DAT, UCEED, or state-level design tests.

4

Appear for Entrance Exams

Attempt design aptitude tests, creative drawing rounds, written exams, and personal interviews as per college requirements.

5

Apply to Colleges

Fill application forms for fashion institutes and universities. Apply through entrance exams or direct admission routes.

6

Counseling & Seat Allotment

Participate in counseling, portfolio reviews, and seat allotment rounds based on rank, creativity, and merit.

7

Document Verification

Submit academic certificates, entrance scorecards, portfolio, ID proof, and other required documents.

8

Enrollment & Classes

Complete fee payment, attend orientation, and begin your fashion design journey with studio work and industry exposure.

Important Entrance Exams for Fashion Design

NIFT Entrance Exam

Conducted by the National Institute of Fashion Technology for UG and PG fashion design programs.

NID DAT

Design Aptitude Test for admission to National Institute of Design programs.

UCEED

Undergraduate Common Entrance Exam for Design accepted by select design colleges.

University / State Design Exams

Private universities and state institutes conduct their own entrance tests or offer merit-based admissions.
