Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about yoga courses, certifications, and career opportunities.

Q. What is the eligibility for B.Sc Yoga in India?

You should have completed 10+2 with any stream. Some institutes may prefer students with prior yoga experience. Age limits vary by institute.

Q. Which entrance exams are required for yoga courses?

Some institutes conduct their own entrance tests or interviews. Ministry of AYUSH certified courses may require assessment for teacher training programs.

Q. Do you provide yoga admission counseling?

Yes, we provide expert guidance for yoga course selection, application assistance, document verification, and admission support.

Q. What career opportunities are available after completing yoga courses?

Graduates can become Yoga Instructors, Wellness Coaches, Therapists, Yoga Therapists, or start their own yoga studio. Higher studies include M.Sc Yoga or specialized training.

Q. Are scholarships available for yoga students?

Yes, institutes and government bodies offer merit-based scholarships, AYUSH scholarships, and support for SC/ST/OBC students.

Q. Can I pursue yoga courses without prior experience?

Yes, many diploma and degree programs accept beginners. Teacher Training Certification (TTC) courses often start from basic levels.

Reputed institutes include Swami Vivekananda Yoga Anusandhan Sansthan (S-VYASA), Morarji Desai National Institute of Yoga, Patanjali University, Amrita University, and Jain Vishva Bharati University.
