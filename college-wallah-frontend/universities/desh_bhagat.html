Desh Bhagat University Overview

Desh Bhagat University (DBU) is a prominent private university located in Mandi Gobindgarh, Punjab. The university is committed to delivering high-quality education across engineering, management, pharmacy, law, and allied disciplines. DBU focuses on skill development, innovation, and industry-ready graduates.

Approved by the UGC and recognized by professional bodies like AICTE, DBU offers comprehensive undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs. The campus boasts state-of-the-art labs, libraries, sports facilities, and strong industry collaborations to foster practical learning and career opportunities.

DBU's placement and training department works closely with leading companies to provide internships, training programs, and job placements. The university also promotes research, entrepreneurship, and community service through specialized centers and student activities.

Eligibility Criteria

Eligibility depends on program; typical requirements are listed below.

Undergraduate Programs

  • B.Tech: 10+2 with PCM, typically minimum 50% marks; entrance: JEE Main or university test.
  • B.Pharm: 10+2 with PCB/PCM, minimum marks as specified; entrance: university or state entrance.
  • B.Arch: 10+2 with Mathematics and NATA/architecture entrance.
  • LLB: Graduation with minimum 45% marks; entrance: CLAT or university test.

Postgraduate Programs

  • M.Tech: B.Tech/B.E in relevant branch; entrance: GATE or university test.
  • MBA: Graduation with minimum 50% marks; entrance: CAT/MAT/CMAT or university test.
  • M.Pharm: B.Pharm with required minimum percentage; entrance: GPAT or university exam.
  • LLM: LLB with minimum 50% marks; entrance: university or state exam.

* Relaxations follow government and university norms. Contact admissions for precise eligibility and entrance requirements.

Admission Process

Follow these general steps to apply to Desh Bhagat University:

  1. Application: Fill online application or visit the admissions office.
  2. Entrance/Eligibility: Appear for required entrance tests or provide qualifying exam scores.
  3. Shortlisting: Merit list prepared based on scores and eligibility.
  4. Counseling & Document Verification: Attend counseling and submit originals.
  5. Fee Payment: Pay fees to confirm admission.

* International applicants should check visa and additional documentation requirements.
