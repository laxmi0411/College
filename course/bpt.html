Bachelor of Physiotherapy (BPT)

Comprehensive Program Details, Syllabus, Career, and Admission

About BPT

Bachelor of Physiotherapy (BPT) is a 4.5-year undergraduate program, including 6 months of mandatory internship, that trains students in the science of physical movement and therapeutic exercises. Physiotherapy helps patients restore, maintain, and maximize strength, function, and overall well-being after injury, illness, or surgery.

Duration

The BPT course is 4 years of academic study + 6 months of compulsory internship.

Eligibility

Students must have completed 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, and Biology (PCB) with at least 50% marks. Admission is usually through entrance exams like NEET, IPU CET, or state-level tests.

Syllabus Overview

  • 1st Year: Anatomy, Physiology, Biochemistry, Biomechanics, Psychology
  • 2nd Year: Pathology, Microbiology, Pharmacology, Exercise Therapy, Electrotherapy
  • 3rd Year: General Medicine, General Surgery, Orthopedics, Neurology, Community Medicine
  • 4th Year: Physiotherapy in Orthopedics, Neurology, Cardio-Respiratory, Pediatrics, Rehabilitation
  • Internship: Clinical training in hospitals and rehabilitation centers

Career Opportunities

  • Physiotherapist in Hospitals & Clinics
  • Sports Rehabilitation Specialist
  • Neuro & Ortho Rehabilitation
  • Pediatric & Geriatric Physiotherapy
  • Research & Teaching
  • Private Practice

Higher Studies

  • Master of Physiotherapy (MPT)
  • Specializations: Orthopedics, Neurology, Cardio-Respiratory, Sports, Pediatrics
  • PhD in Physiotherapy
  • Fellowship & Diploma Programs

Admission Process

Admission is based on entrance exams (NEET, CETs) or merit in qualifying exams. Candidates must clear counseling and submit required documents. Some universities also conduct personal interviews.

Top Colleges in India for BPT

  • Manipal College of Health Professions, Manipal
  • Christian Medical College (CMC), Vellore
  • All India Institute of Physical Medicine & Rehabilitation, Mumbai
  • Amity Institute of Physiotherapy, Noida
  • Jamia Hamdard University, New Delhi
  • Rajiv Gandhi University of Health Sciences, Bangalore

Average Salary

Fresh graduates can expect ₹2.5 – ₹5 LPA. With experience and specialization, salaries can reach ₹8 – ₹12 LPA, especially in private hospitals and sports institutions.

Skills Required

  • Strong knowledge of anatomy & physiology
  • Communication & empathy
  • Critical thinking & diagnosis
  • Manual therapy & exercise prescription
  • Patience and problem-solving
