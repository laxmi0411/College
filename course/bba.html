Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA)

Program Overview

BBA (Bachelor of Business Administration) is a 3-year undergraduate program focused on management, leadership, and business administration. It is an ideal course for students aiming for corporate, entrepreneurship, and managerial roles.

BBA Syllabus (By Semester)

Semester 1

  • Principles of Management
  • Financial Accounting
  • Business Economics
  • Business Mathematics

Semester 2

  • Marketing Management
  • Business Statistics
  • Business Communication
  • Environmental Studies

Semester 3

  • Human Resource Management
  • Business Law
  • Management Accounting
  • Organizational Behaviour

Semester 4

  • Financial Management
  • Research Methodology
  • Operations Management
  • Business Ethics

Semester 5

  • Strategic Management
  • International Business
  • Entrepreneurship Development
  • Elective I

Semester 6

  • Project Work
  • Business Policy
  • Elective II
  • Leadership Skills

Career Opportunities after BBA

  • Marketing Executive
  • HR Manager
  • Financial Analyst
  • Business Consultant
  • Entrepreneur
