Key Entrance Exams 2026

JEE Main

Mandatory for NITs, IIITs, and CFTIs. Conducted twice a year by NTA.

JEE Advanced

Exclusively for IIT admissions. Requires high percentile in JEE Main.

State CETs

State-specific tests like WBJEE, MHT-CET, and KCET for regional colleges.

BITSAT

Premier computer-based test for BITS Pilani, Goa, and Hyderabad campuses.

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common queries about admissions and careers.

Q. What is the eligibility for B.Tech in India?

10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics. Minimum 75% for IITs/NITs; 45-50% for most private universities. Age: 17-25 years.

Q. Which entrance exams are required?

JEE Main (NITs/IIITs), JEE Advanced (IITs), State CETs (WBJEE, MHT-CET), and University exams like BITSAT or VITEEE.

Q. Can I get admission without JEE?

Yes, many private universities offer admission based on 10+2 marks or their own entrance tests. Lateral entry is also available for diploma holders.

Q. What are the career prospects after B.Tech?

Roles include Software/Civil/Mechanical Engineer, Data Scientist, or Analyst. Options for further study include M.Tech or MBA.
