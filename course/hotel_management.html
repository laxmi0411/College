Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common queries about admissions and careers.

Q. What is the eligibility for BHM in India?

10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics/Biology. Minimum 50% for government institutes; 45-50% for private universities.

Q. Which entrance exam is required?

NCHMCT JEE for government institutes, state CETs, and university-specific entrance tests for private colleges.

Q. Can I get admission without NCHMCT JEE?

Yes, many private universities offer admission based on 10+2 marks or their own entrance tests. Direct admission is also available.

Q. What are the career prospects after BHM?

Roles include Hotel Manager, Restaurant Manager, Event Coordinator, Airline Catering Manager, or Entrepreneurship.
