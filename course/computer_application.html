National level entrance exam for engineering programs. Conducted twice a year.

National level entrance exam for MCA programs. Conducted by NITs.

State CETs

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about computer application admissions, courses, and career prospects.

What is the eligibility for BCA in India?

You need to have completed 10+2 with Mathematics with minimum 50% marks. Age should be between 17-23 years. English must be a subject in 10+2. Some colleges may require Computer Science as an additional subject.

Which entrance exam is required for MCA?

NIMCET is the main entrance exam for MCA programs in NITs. State CETs like MAH MCA CET, UPSEE, etc., are required for state colleges. Some universities conduct their own entrance exams or offer merit-based admission.

Do you help with computer application admission counseling?

Yes, we provide free counseling for computer application courses. Our experts help with college selection, entrance exam preparation, document verification, and admission process guidance.

What are the job opportunities after BCA?

Graduates can work as Software Developer, System Analyst, Database Administrator, Web Developer, IT Support Specialist, and in various IT companies. Higher studies options include MCA and MBA IT.

Are there scholarships available for computer application students?

Yes, various scholarships are available including merit-based scholarships, state government scholarships, and those for SC/ST/OBC students. Private colleges also offer merit scholarships and fee waivers.
