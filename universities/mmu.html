Maharishi Markandeshwar University Overview

Maharishi Markandeshwar University (MMU) is a deemed university located in Mullana, Haryana, India. Established in 1993, the university is committed to providing quality education in various fields including engineering, medical sciences, management, and humanities. MMU is recognized for its state-of-the-art infrastructure, experienced faculty, and focus on holistic development.

Approved by the University Grants Commission (UGC), MMU offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs. The university emphasizes research, innovation, and industry collaboration to prepare students for global challenges. With modern classrooms, advanced laboratories, libraries, and sports facilities, MMU provides an enriching academic environment.

MMU's placement cell works tirelessly to connect students with top companies, ensuring excellent career opportunities. The university also promotes extracurricular activities, cultural events, and community service to foster well-rounded personalities.

Eligibility Criteria

Admission to MMU requires meeting specific academic and entrance exam criteria. Details are as follows:

Undergraduate Programs

  • B.Tech: 10+2 with PCM (Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics) with minimum 50% marks. Entrance: JEE Main or university entrance test.
  • MBBS: 10+2 with PCB (Physics, Chemistry, Biology) with minimum 50% marks. Entrance: NEET UG.
  • BDS: 10+2 with PCB with minimum 50% marks. Entrance: NEET UG.
  • B.Sc Nursing: 10+2 with PCB with minimum 50% marks. Entrance: University test or merit-based.
  • B.Pharm: 10+2 with PCM/PCB with minimum 50% marks. Entrance: Merit-based or university test.
  • BBA: 10+2 in any stream with minimum 50% marks. No entrance required; merit-based admission.
  • BCA: 10+2 with Mathematics as a subject, minimum 50% marks. Merit-based or entrance test.
  • BA/B.Sc/B.Com: 10+2 in relevant stream with minimum 50% marks. Merit-based admission.
  • LLB: Graduation in any discipline with minimum 50% marks. Entrance: CLAT or university test.
  • BPT/BOT: 10+2 with PCB with minimum 50% marks. Merit-based admission.

Postgraduate Programs

  • M.Tech: B.Tech/B.E in relevant branch with minimum 55% marks. Entrance: GATE or university test.
  • MD/MS: MBBS with internship completion. Entrance: NEET PG.
  • MDS: BDS with internship completion. Entrance: NEET MDS.
  • M.Sc Nursing: B.Sc Nursing with minimum 55% marks. Entrance: University test.
  • M.Pharm: B.Pharm with minimum 55% marks. Entrance: GPAT or university test.
  • MBA: Bachelor's degree in any discipline with minimum 50% marks. Entrance: CAT/MAT/CMAT or university test.
  • MCA: Bachelor's degree with Mathematics, minimum 50% marks. Entrance: Merit-based or university test.
  • MA/M.Sc/M.Com: Bachelor's degree in relevant field with minimum 50% marks. Merit-based.
  • LLM: LLB with minimum 55% marks. Entrance: CLAT PG or university test.
  • MPT/MOT: BPT/BOT with minimum 50% marks. Merit-based.

Doctoral Programs

  • Ph.D: Master's degree in relevant field with minimum 55% marks. Entrance: UGC NET/GATE or university entrance test followed by interview.

* Relaxation in marks for reserved categories as per government norms. Age limit: 17-25 for UG, no upper limit for PG/Ph.D.

Admission Process

The admission process at MMU is streamlined to ensure a smooth experience for students. Follow these steps:

  1. Application: Fill the online application form on the university website or visit the campus. Provide personal, academic, and contact details.
  2. Entrance Exam: Appear for the required entrance exam (NEET, JEE, CAT, etc.) or university test if applicable.
  3. Merit List: Based on exam scores and 10+2/graduation marks, a merit list is prepared.
  4. Counseling: Shortlisted candidates attend counseling sessions for course and branch selection.
  5. Document Verification: Submit original documents including mark sheets, certificates, ID proof, and photos.
  6. Fee Payment: Pay the admission fee and first-year tuition fee to confirm seat.
  7. Orientation: Attend the orientation program to familiarize with the campus and curriculum.

* For international students, additional requirements like visa, passport, and English proficiency test (IELTS/TOEFL) may apply. Contact admissions office for detailed guidance.
