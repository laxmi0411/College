SRM University Overview

SRM Institute of Science and Technology (SRMIST) is a private deemed university located in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India. Established in 1985, SRM has grown into one of India's premier educational institutions, offering a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs across disciplines like engineering, medicine, management, law, science, and humanities.

Recognized by the University Grants Commission (UGC) and accredited by NAAC with 'A++' grade, SRM adheres to high educational standards. The university boasts state-of-the-art infrastructure, including modern classrooms, advanced laboratories, a vast library, sports facilities, and extensive hostel accommodations.

SRM emphasizes research, innovation, and industry collaboration, providing students with opportunities for internships, projects, and global exposure through partnerships with leading companies and universities worldwide.

The university's dedicated placement cell ensures excellent career opportunities, with top companies recruiting students for high-paying roles. SRM aims to foster holistic development, combining academic rigor with extracurricular activities to prepare students for global challenges.

Eligibility Criteria

Admission to SRM University requires meeting specific academic and entrance exam criteria. Details are as follows:

Undergraduate Programs

  • B.Tech: 10+2 with PCM (Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics) with minimum 50% marks (45% for reserved categories). Entrance: SRM Joint Engineering Entrance Examination (SRMJEEE) or JEE Main.
  • MBBS: 10+2 with PCB (Physics, Chemistry, Biology) with minimum 50% marks. Entrance: NEET UG.
  • BDS: 10+2 with PCB with minimum 50% marks. Entrance: NEET UG.
  • B.Arch: 10+2 with Mathematics and minimum 50% marks. Entrance: NATA or SRM's entrance test.
  • BBA: 10+2 in any stream with minimum 50% marks. Entrance: SRM's entrance test or merit-based.
  • BCA: 10+2 with Mathematics as a subject, minimum 50% marks. Entrance: Merit-based or SRM's entrance test.
  • BA/B.Sc/B.Com: 10+2 in relevant stream with minimum 50% marks. Merit-based admission.
  • LLB: Graduation in any discipline with minimum 50% marks. Entrance: CLAT or SRM's entrance test.
  • B.Pharm: 10+2 with PCM/PCB, minimum 50% marks. Entrance: SRM's entrance test or merit-based.
  • BPT: 10+2 with PCB, minimum 50% marks. Merit-based admission.

Postgraduate Programs

  • M.Tech: B.Tech/B.E in relevant branch with minimum 50% marks. Entrance: GATE or SRM's entrance test.
  • MD/MS: MBBS with internship completion. Entrance: NEET PG.
  • MDS: BDS with internship completion. Entrance: NEET MDS.
  • MBA: Bachelor's degree in any discipline with minimum 50% marks. Entrance: CAT/MAT/CMAT/GMAT or SRM's entrance test.
  • MCA: Bachelor's degree with Mathematics, minimum 50% marks. Entrance: Merit-based or SRM's entrance test.
  • MA/M.Sc/M.Com: Bachelor's degree in relevant field with minimum 50% marks. Merit-based.
  • LLM: LLB with minimum 50% marks. Entrance: CLAT PG or SRM's entrance test.
  • M.Pharm: B.Pharm with minimum 50% marks. Entrance: GPAT or SRM's entrance test.
  • MPT: BPT with minimum 50% marks. Merit-based admission.

Doctoral Programs

  • Ph.D: Master's degree in relevant field with minimum 55% marks. Entrance: UGC NET/CSIR NET/GATE or SRM's entrance test followed by interview.

* Relaxation in marks for reserved categories as per government norms. Age limit: 17 years minimum for UG medical courses, no upper limit for PG/Ph.D.

Admission Process

The admission process at SRM University is streamlined to ensure a smooth experience for students. Follow these steps:

  1. Application: Fill the online application form on the SRM website or through the admission portal. Provide personal, academic, and contact details.
  2. Entrance Exam: Appear for the required entrance exam (SRMJEEE, NEET, CAT, etc.) or SRM's own test if applicable.
  3. Merit List: Based on exam scores, academic performance, and other criteria, a merit list is prepared.
  4. Counseling: Shortlisted candidates attend counseling sessions for course and specialization selection.
  5. Document Verification: Submit original documents including mark sheets, certificates, ID proof, and photos.
  6. Fee Payment: Pay the admission fee and first installment to confirm seat.
  7. Orientation: Attend the orientation program to familiarize with the campus and curriculum.

* For international students, additional requirements like visa, passport, and English proficiency test (IELTS/TOEFL) may apply. Contact admissions office for detailed guidance.
