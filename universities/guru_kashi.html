Guru Kashi University Overview

Guru Kashi University (GKU) is a prominent private university located in Talwandi Sabo, Punjab. The university is committed to delivering high-quality education across engineering, agriculture, management, pharmacy, law, and allied disciplines. GKU focuses on skill development, innovation, and industry-ready graduates.

Approved by the UGC and recognized by professional bodies like AICTE, GKU offers comprehensive undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs. The campus boasts state-of-the-art labs, libraries, sports facilities, and strong industry collaborations to foster practical learning and career opportunities.

GKU's placement and training department works closely with leading companies to provide internships, training programs, and job placements. The university also promotes research, entrepreneurship, and community service through specialized centers and student activities.
